Forest

Community Tree Planting Day

April 28, 2025
9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Greenwood Park, 123 Nature Ave, Portland

About This Event

Join us for a day of reforestation as we plant 500 native trees to restore our local ecosystem. This event is suitable for all ages and experience levels. We'll provide all necessary equipment, training, and refreshments. Come dressed for outdoor work and weather conditions.

Your participation helps combat climate change, restore wildlife habitats, prevent soil erosion, and beautify our community spaces.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothes that can get dirty
  • Sturdy closed-toe shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Work gloves (if you have them)

We'll Provide

  • Tree saplings and planting materials
  • Shovels, gloves, and tools
  • Training and guidance
  • Light refreshments and water
  • Participation certificates
