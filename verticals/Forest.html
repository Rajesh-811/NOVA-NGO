Protecting Earth's Lungs

Forests are the lungs of our Earth, providing essential resources, supporting biodiversity, regulating the climate, and sustaining life. At NOVA NGO, we are deeply committed to protecting, restoring, and expanding these vital ecosystems through awareness, action, and sustainable practices.

Biodiversity Conservation

Our initiatives focus on preserving diverse species of flora and fauna, maintaining ecological balance, and protecting endangered species in their natural habitats.

Community Empowerment

We work closely with local communities, providing training in sustainable forestry practices and creating economic opportunities through forest conservation.

Climate Action

Through reforestation and forest protection, we contribute to carbon sequestration and climate change mitigation, making a real impact on global environmental health.

Our Programs

We implement a variety of programs aimed at forest conservation, community development, and environmental education.

Tree Plantation Drives

Regular community-based tree planting initiatives to restore forest cover and create green spaces in urban and rural areas. We organize monthly plantation drives with local schools, businesses, and community groups.

  • Over 5,000 saplings planted monthly
  • Native species selection for biodiversity
  • 3-year monitoring program

Community Training

Empowering local communities with skills in agroforestry, organic farming, and sustainable harvesting of forest resources. Our expert trainers provide hands-on workshops and long-term support.

  • Weekly workshops in 45 villages
  • Sustainable income generation
  • Expert mentorship program

Environmental Education

Awareness campaigns and educational programs for schools and communities about the importance of forest conservation. We provide interactive learning experiences through field visits and practical demonstrations.

  • 200+ schools participated
  • Youth leadership programs
  • Monthly nature workshops

Community Engagement

We actively engage local communities, especially rural farmers and women's groups, by training them in eco-friendly livelihoods. By doing so, we not only restore green cover but also improve economic opportunities.

Agroforestry Initiatives

Training farmers to integrate trees into agricultural landscapes, improving soil health and creating sustainable income sources.

Women's Empowerment

Special programs for women's groups focused on medicinal plant cultivation and forest-based micro-enterprises.

Sustainable Farming

Promoting organic farming practices that reduce chemical use and preserve biodiversity in agricultural landscapes.
