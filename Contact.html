Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with NOVA NGO?

You can volunteer by filling out our volunteer application form on the Volunteer page, or by contacting us directly. We have various opportunities available, from one-time events to ongoing commitments.

How are donations used?

Your donations directly support our community programs, educational initiatives, and operational costs. We ensure that at least 85% of all donations go directly to our program services.

Can my organization partner with NOVA NGO?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with businesses, schools, and other organizations. Please contact us to discuss potential collaboration opportunities that align with our mission.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

We offer internships for students and recent graduates in various fields. Internships typically last 3-6 months and provide valuable hands-on experience in the non-profit sector.
